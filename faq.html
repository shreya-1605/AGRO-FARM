Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?

A: You can place an order by browsing our products and adding them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Please check the product page for specific details.

Q: How do I contact customer support?

A: You can contact us via WhatsApp or email at support@agrofarm.com.
